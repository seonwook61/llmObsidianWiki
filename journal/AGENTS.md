# Journal Policy

`journal/` is the private capture layer.

## Purpose

Record daily work, study notes, friction points, and ideas before they are filtered into durable knowledge or public writing.

## Rules

- Notes here are private by default.
- Use daily notes for time-based capture.
- Use topic notes for ongoing private working notes per domain.
- Do not treat journal files as publish-ready content.
- Public posts must be intentionally derived into `publish/posts/`.
- Link journal notes to relevant `wiki/` pages when a captured idea becomes durable knowledge.

## Promotion Criteria

Promote a journal insight into a public post only when at least one of these is true:

- It teaches a reusable lesson.
- It documents an implementation, bug, workflow, or decision that others could benefit from.
- It can be grounded in sources, knowledge pages, or a clear firsthand experience.
