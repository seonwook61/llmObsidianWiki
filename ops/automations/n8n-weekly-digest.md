# n8n Weekly Digest

## Trigger

- Schedule once per week

## Input

- `publish/posts/` status summary
- `publish/metadata/` review dates
- optional Codex audit output

## Output

- a compact weekly digest to Notion, email, or chat
- counts for drafts, reviews, published posts, and stale posts

## Guardrails

- Do not rewrite repository files
- Do not promote drafts automatically
